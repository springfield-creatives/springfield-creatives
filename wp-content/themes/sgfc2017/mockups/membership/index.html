Why sponsor Springfield Creatives?

Why join Springfield Creatives? To build the scene that will shape your career opportunities in Springfield. As a member, you’ll have a place to shake hands with other creative collaborators and industry leaders, and hear firsthand about what‘s happening in the creative community. Plus, we provide regular curated content in our meetings, events and newsletters that are designed to make you better at what you do, whatever you do.

Why It’s Worth It

Meetings and Special Events

You’ll be able to attend any SGFC meeting or special event for free. Content is curated to provide you with the best opportunties to meet other members, learn something new, or hear from national speakers like Aaron Draplin and Quinn Norton.

Find A Job

Visit our jobs board for current listings for part time, full time and contract or freelance gigs. Set your profile as “Accepting new projects” if you’re open to inquiries, and attend meetings and events to hear about opportunties firsthand and build your own network of collaborators and referal sources.

Directory Listing

Your profile will be listed in our member directory, where you can share your portfolio, contact and share when you’re accepting new gigs or projects for prospective clients and emplooyers to view. We encourage employers, clients and business leaders in the community to make this their first stop when looking for creative help.

Collboration

Where else in Springfield will you find designers, developers, photographers, podcasters, creative directors, furniture makers, students, illustrators, writers, project managers, videographers, animators, and editors all in the same room? Check out our member directory next time you need to find a project collaborator.

Perks

By being a member of Springfield Creatives, you’ll be eligible for discounts or other perks at several local businesses.

Experience

If you’re looking for a way to gain real-world experience in design, development, photography, communications, writing, or event planning, our committee work provides a structured means to do so, with a not-so-big time commitment.

Leadership

One step into a Springfield Creatives meeting or event, and you’ll see that we’re a hotbed of local creative leadership, and our structure works to facilitate personal growth and leadership opportunities. It’s definitely somethign you want to get in on. Don’t see your field covered enough in SGFC topics? We’ll help you get something started to change that.

Professional Development

Our Meetings and Events committees are constantly working to provide new content designed to facilitate personal and professional growth in your field, from workshops about building your portfolio to skill-broadening tutorials from local pros.

Sign Me Up

Are You A Current Student

Then we have some good news for you. Your membership to Springfield Creatives will be free for as long as you’re enrolled in current classes. One of our biggest goals is to help you bridge the gap between your student life today and the professional world that you’ll soon be stepping into. We know you’re busy, but trust us that it’s worth it to get involved now, make connections and get a step ahead in the creative community. Here are a few things you can do in the group to get more involved.

Find A Mentor

Contact our Students committee to get signed up in our mentorship program, where we connect you with local pros in your field to meet and have a channel for ongoing feedback and guidance throughout the school year. You can also use our membership directory to search for members open to mentorship and reach out on your own.

Join A Committee

For an easy way to get plugged in that doesn’t involve any awkward mingling or networking, we really suggest joining any of our committees. It’s a great way to meet other members, and you can also use committee projects to gain experience or portfolio material in whatever your field. We need designers, developers, writers, illustrators, photographers, project managers and more.

Portfolio Review

We offer an annual portfolio review for students and job-seeking members to haul in their books, laptops, and resumés to get direct and friendly feedback from a wide range of pros in Springfield Creatives. Keep your eye out for an announcement in the early Spring.

Job Shadow

We’re working to expand a list of local business opening their doors occasionally to show students the ropes for a day or half a day in a variety of fields.
