Why sponsor Springfield Creatives?

A paragraph that less focuses on tangibles, although they will be listed below, but more that we really do need the financial support and investment from local businesses to keep our mission alive and foster the continued collaboration between us and you. If you believe in the things we’re doing, we need you to step up and support what we’re doing.

Why It’s Worth It

Better Hires

We are working to find as many ways as possible to allow you to engage with a motivated and plugged-in talent pool. With Springfield Creatives, you’ll have a direct line to the best local talent, and up-and-coming student membership that will soon be entering the job market in search of jobs and internships.

Featured Jobs

Posting on our jobs board is now free for all, but our sponsored members receive featured job postings for maximum reach to our engaged membership, whether you’re looking for a full time, part time, or contract gig employee.

Get The Word Out

Many of our sponsorship levels come with opportunity to promote your business to our membership through title sponsorships of meetings or special events, and featured articles or job postings in our monthly newsletter.

Feel Goods

We have to say, we think you’ll feel really great about supporting our efforts to make Springfield a better place to be a creative. Not only will it be a mutually beneficial partnership, it’ll be beneficial for Springfield and is helping to move things in a new direction. This is exciting stuff!
